Wondering what Your Notes is?

Your Notes is a web application which you can use to store simple notes such as todo lists.

Reconsidering giving out Your Data?

The application runs on Your Browser and this means that the information you provide during Registration or while using the app does not get collected by me or anyone else.

It all get's stored locally in your browser.

Getting started with Your Notes

As you've probably noticed, you need to Register to use the app.

When you're ready, click the Register button above.

Important!

Remember your Registration username and password. You'll need it for Login

After registration, click the Login button above.

Next Steps!

A successful Login will take you to Your Dashboard. A section of the app where notes you create will appear. Click the Create New button to get started.
